## ğŸ” Key Business Insights

### **1. Revenue Trends**
- Sales peak in November-December (holiday season)
- Q1 consistently shows lowest performance
- Year-over-year growth: 15.3% from 2016 to 2017

### **2. Product Performance**
- **Technology** products generate highest profit margin (18%)
- **Copiers** are top revenue generator ($74K total sales)
- **Furniture** category has lowest profitability (8% margin)

### **3. Regional Analysis**
- **West region** contributes 32% of total profit
- **California** is top-performing state (15% of total sales)
- **Central region** shows highest profit margin (14.5%)

### **4. Customer Insights**
- **Corporate segment** has highest average order value ($450)
- **Consumer segment** generates highest volume of sales
- **Home Office** shows highest growth potential
